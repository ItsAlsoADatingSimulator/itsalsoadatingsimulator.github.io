Project Date is an in-progress dating sim Visual Novel focusing on members of the /r/RWBY and RWBYC community.

You are a new person to the community, undergoing the typical initiation process and observing the activities and subject matters that occur in /r/RWBY and RWBYC. You contribute little by little until your new friends get to know you. Some of them are eccentric but likeable, others like to bicker, and some even lurk only saying a word or two. Desperate to get to know these interesting strangers, you find that they all live in the same town. Your goal is to meet them in real life in order to find out if you'll end up being more than just friends.
